About Me

Engineer by training.
Explorer by nature.

I'm a robotics researcher with a passion for both intelligent machines and the open road. B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Gandhinagar (minor in Robotics), now pursuing an MRes in Design Engineering at Imperial College London.

Over the years I've worked across cities and disciplines — from Pune to Seattle — blending robotics, AI, and computer vision to build useful, adaptive systems. This site represents not just my work, but a way of working: traveling light, thinking deep, solving problems wherever they arise.
